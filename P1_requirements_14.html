Our Client

  • Client Name: Wingsuits (band)
  • Contact Person: Jørgen Thorsnes

Our client, Wingsuits, is a band based in Trondheim. They are five members and they play indie/pop music. They have a basic website already, but want a more extensive website with more information. We aim to create this website. Our contact person, Jørgen, will be representing the band, as well as being a part of our team.

Purpose and Goals

The site’s purpose is to convey information about the band Wingsuits. The band wishes to be acknowledged internationally, meaning the site will be written in English. The band want the website to promote and inform about concerts, themselves, their music, and also present images and music videos.

Audience

Wingsuits is a norwegian indiepop/rock band, based in Trondheim. People interested in this musical genre, people from Trondheim and people who enjoy listening to norwegian bands will like to visit Wingsuits website to learn more about the band. People who already like the band will want to visit the website to learn when and where their next concert is due, they can visit to buy merchandise or to come in contact with the band. Both fans and people trying to book the band can come in contact with Wingsuits through their website. People who want to view photos, videos or listen to Wingsuits music can get all of the above easily from their website.

Specific Preferences

  • Wingsuits would like the website to have clean and simple look.
  • Nan Nan would like to have her artwork displayed in sections.
  • Nan Nan and Geir Henbest have specifically told us that they would like to be able to modify the content of the website. Thus, we plan to use Javascript to write the content.
  • The content of the website should emphasize the paintings and sculpture.
  • If possible, our client would like to include some content in the local language, although this is not a requirement.

Final Location of the Website

Geir Henbest will donate the domain name and the hosting fees. He has informed us that the domain name will eventually be www.nannanartstudio.com.

Technical Difficulties

We plan to post many pictures in the gallery sections of our website, so we may run into the technical problem regarding slowly-loading webpages. Such a website will be frustrating for users, so we will need to make sure to avoid such a problem by being conscious about the size and number of the photographs we use.
